Research

LIGHT · ENERGY · MOTION, designed with intelligence

Soft-material devices that sense their environment and reshape themselves: from strain-tunable structural colors to smart windows, artificial muscles, and AI-optimized designs.

Light

Tunable Photonic Devices 기계변색 신축성 포토닉스 · 가변 광학 필터 · 파장 가변 레이저 · 액정 기반 실리콘 포토닉스

Chiral liquid crystal photonics whose reflection and lasing wavelengths tune with strain, field, and temperature, plus nW-power LC-integrated silicon photonics.
Energy

Smart Windows & Transmission Control 적외선 선택 반사 스마트 윈도우 · 투과도 가변 광소자

IR-selective reflection and guest-host dimming devices for adaptive thermal management and light control.
Motion

Soft Actuators & Robotics 소프트 액추에이터 및 로보틱스

DEA, LCE, and PVDF artificial-muscle platforms for soft grippers, wearable robots, and medical microactuators.
Intelligence

Simulation & AI-Driven Optimization 멀티피직스 시뮬레이션 및 AI 기반 소자 최적화

FDTD, multiphysics FEA, and machine learning combined into experiment–simulation feedback loops for inverse design.
